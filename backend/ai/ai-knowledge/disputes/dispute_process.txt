Dispute Handling Process:

A dispute can be raised if:
- Work quality is unsatisfactory.
- Deadline is missed.
- Payment disagreement occurs.

Dispute Steps:
1. User raises dispute.
2. Admin reviews project details.
3. Admin reviews chat logs and submission.
4. Admin makes final decision.

Possible Outcomes:
- Refund to client.
- Partial payment.
- Full payment to freelancer.
