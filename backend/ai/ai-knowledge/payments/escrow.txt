Escrow System in SkillNest:

SkillNest follows a milestone-based escrow concept.

How It Works:
1. Client agrees to hire a student.
2. Payment is reserved for the project.
3. Student completes the work.
4. Client approves submission.
5. Payment is released to student.

Purpose:
- Protect clients from incomplete work.
- Protect students from non-payment.
- Ensure trust between both parties.

Escrow ensures secure and structured transactions.
