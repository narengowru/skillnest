Withdrawal Policy – SkillNest

Eligibility:
A freelancer can request withdrawal only when:
- The project status is marked as "Completed".
- The client has approved the final submission.
- Payment is successfully received in escrow.

Processing Time:
- Withdrawal requests are processed within 7 working days.
- Processing time may vary during weekends or public holidays.

Minimum Withdrawal:
- A minimum withdrawal amount may apply as per platform policy.
- Requests below the minimum threshold will be rejected.

Withdrawal Status Types:
1. Pending – Request submitted and under review.
2. Approved – Request verified and scheduled for processing.
3. Processed – Payment successfully transferred.
4. Failed – Payment transfer failed due to incorrect details or bank issues.

Failure Conditions:
Withdrawal may fail if:
- Incorrect bank details are provided.
- Account verification is incomplete.
- Suspicious activity is detected.

Security Checks:
- All withdrawals undergo verification checks.
- Platform may temporarily hold funds if fraud is suspected.

Important:
- Commission (10%) is deducted before withdrawal.
- SkillNest reserves the right to delay withdrawal in case of dispute.
