Project Capacity Limiter Policy – SkillNest

Purpose:
SkillNest ensures fair opportunity distribution among students.

Rule:
A student can work on a maximum of 2 active projects per month.

Reason:
- Prevents overloading.
- Encourages equal project access.
- Maintains quality delivery.
- Gives new students opportunity.

What Counts as Active Project:
- Project status = Accepted
- Project status = In Progress

Once project is completed, it no longer counts toward the limit.

If limit is reached:
- Student cannot accept new projects until next month.
