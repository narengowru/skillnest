Guidance for Students Not Getting Projects

If a student is not getting projects, possible reasons include:

1. Incomplete profile
2. Low rating
3. No portfolio items
4. Low-quality proposals
5. Slow response time
6. Applying for highly competitive projects

Improvement Suggestions:
- Add more skills relevant to market demand.
- Upload academic or personal projects.
- Write stronger proposals.
- Respond quickly to client messages.
- Maintain professional communication.
- Improve rating by completing projects successfully.

Motivational Message:
Freelancing takes time. Early consistency builds long-term success.
Focus on improving skills and profile quality.
