SkillNest is a student-focused freelancing marketplace.

Purpose:
SkillNest connects students and recent graduates with businesses, startups, and individuals who need short-term freelance services.

Target Users:
- Students (Freelancers)
- Clients (Businesses / Individuals)
- Admin

Core Idea:
Unlike general freelancing platforms, SkillNest is designed specifically for beginners and early-career freelancers.

Key Features:
- Skill-based student profiles
- Project posting by clients
- Project bidding system
- Real-time messaging
- Portfolio showcasing
- Review and rating system
- Student verification system

Mission:
To bridge the gap between education and industry by helping students gain real-world experience, build portfolios, and earn income.
