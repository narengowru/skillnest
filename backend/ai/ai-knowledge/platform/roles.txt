There are three main roles in SkillNest:

1. Student (Freelancer):
- Creates a skill-based profile.
- Adds portfolio and academic projects.
- Browses available projects.
- Submits bids to projects.
- Communicates with clients.
- Completes assigned work.
- Receives reviews and ratings.

2. Client:
- Creates an account.
- Posts freelance projects with budget and deadline.
- Reviews student profiles.
- Accepts or rejects bids.
- Communicates with selected student.
- Reviews completed work.
- Leaves rating and feedback.

3. Admin:
- Verifies user accounts.
- Monitors disputes.
- Manages reported users.
- Maintains platform integrity.
