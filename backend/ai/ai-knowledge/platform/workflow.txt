SkillNest Workflow:

1. Registration:
Users sign up as Student or Client.
Accounts may require verification.

2. Profile Setup:
Students complete profile with:
- Skills
- Education
- Portfolio
- Certifications

3. Project Posting (Client):
Client posts project including:
- Title
- Description
- Required skills
- Budget
- Deadline

4. Bidding:
Students browse projects.
Students submit bids with proposal and pricing.

5. Selection:
Client reviews bids.
Client accepts one student.

6. Communication:
Client and student communicate through integrated messaging.

7. Completion:
Student submits final work.
Client reviews and approves.

8. Review:
Both parties leave ratings and feedback.

9. Order Status:
Order can be:
- Pending
- Accepted
- Rejected
- Completed
